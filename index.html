  • Ice is a place where you will become not only a little happier but also healthier. Health is the main value for us, and we follow it when creating our handmade desserts.

  • Carefully crafted with traditional recipes and using 100% natural ingredients, our desserts are a delightful and guilt-free indulgence. We take pride in the quality and purity of our products.

  • Every ingredient we use is carefully selected to ensure that it meets our strict standards. From organic fruits to premium nuts and locally-sourced dairy, we prioritize the finest and freshest components in our creations.

  • Children are an essential part of our clientele, and we understand the importance of providing them with safe and healthy treats. That's why our desserts are suitable for children aged three years and above. You can trust that our products are free from any artificial additives or excessive sugars.

  • Range is one thing we excel at. Ice cream enthusiasts will be thrilled with the vast selection of 55 tantalizing ice cream flavors we offer. From classics like chocolate and vanilla to exotic choices like mango passion and salted caramel, there's something to satisfy every palate.

  • Enjoying a cup of coffee at Ice is an experience in itself. With 15 coffee options, including aromatic espressos, velvety cappuccinos, and refreshing iced coffees, our coffee lovers are in for a treat.

  • And for those craving a thick and creamy delight, our milkshakes are a must-try. Indulge in 23 delectable milkshake flavors, ranging from timeless favorites like chocolate and strawberry to innovative combinations like cookies and cream or salted caramel.

  • Meticulously prepared by our skilled artisans, our handmade desserts are a labor of love. Each scoop of ice cream, each sip of coffee, and each sip of a milkshake is a testament to our commitment to quality and taste.

  • Indulge in the delightful world of ICECREAM at Ice, where happiness and health meet in every spoonful and sip.
